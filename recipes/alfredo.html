Absolutely Alfredo Sauce

Description

One of these days, I'm gonna get organezized. But until then, I'll be gulping down this delicious alfredo sauce
I make right in my kitchen here. I think about this stuff day and night, but especially when I have to clean the
backseat of my cab. Hey, it's not so bad. Other nights, I'm cleaning up the blood.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 2 teaspoons garlic minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 cups freshly grated parmesan cheese (not the powdered bullshit)

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in a deep, dark pan.
  2. Pour on that heavy cream.
  3. Stir that around. Get some little bubbles going. Stare at the bubbles until the slow zoom is finished.
  4. Pop those seasonings in there.
  5. Sprinkle and stir the parmesan. Keep doin' it till you have no parmesan left. If you're getting clumpy,
    you don't stir fast enough. You could also use a blender.
  6. Enjoy.
